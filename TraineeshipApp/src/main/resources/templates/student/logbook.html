Tips for keeping a good logbook:
  • Record your daily activities and tasks completed
  • Note any challenges you faced and how you resolved them
  • Document new skills and technologies you're learning
  • Keep track of meetings and important discussions
  • Reflect on your progress and areas for improvement
  • Update regularly - don't leave it until the end!
